1. Who we are

7Captur is an AI decision intelligence service for e-commerce store owners. For any privacy or legal questions, please contact us at: privacy@7captur.com.

2. Privacy & Data Collection

We collect your name, email, and store URL upon inquiry. Once onboarded, we connect to your store platform, ad accounts, and email tools via their official APIs. We access only the data strictly necessary to calculate True ROAS and execute approved campaign changes. We never sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes. Client store data is retained only while your engagement is active and is deleted within 30 days of termination upon request.

3. Ownership & Maintenance

You fully own your Logic configurations and Data Exports. 7Captur retains ownership of the Hosting infrastructure and API Maintenance framework. 7Captur is an Assisted Execution layer. We do not make autonomous changes to your ad accounts.
