The Real Problem

Your store has data.
You just can't act on it.

Four problems every e-commerce store above $10k/mo faces. Sound familiar?

📦
Inventory
"I keep running out of my best sellers."

You're restocking based on gut feel and last month's memory — not on what your actual sell-through rate and supplier lead time are telling you. One stockout on your hero product can cost you two weeks of profit.

💸
Ad Spend
"I have no idea which ads are actually profitable."

Your ad platform shows you a ROAS number. But after platform fees, returns, COGS, and fulfilment — half your "profitable" campaigns are breaking even or worse. You need true profitability, not vanity metrics.

⏱
Time
"I have data but no time to look at it."

You log into five different dashboards, spend 40 minutes, and leave more confused than when you started. These tools were built to show data — not to think for you. That's not a dashboard problem. It's a decisions problem.

⚠️
Costly Mistakes
"I made a call last quarter that cost me."

One wrong restock order or one scaled losing campaign can wipe two weeks of profit. You need a system that flags these before they happen — not a post-mortem three weeks later when the damage is done.

1. Who we are

7Captur is an AI decision intelligence service for e-commerce store owners. We are the data controller for information you provide to us. For any privacy-related questions, contact us at privacy@7captur.com.

2. What data we collect

When you submit a demo request, we collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your store URL and e-commerce platform
  • Your approximate monthly revenue range
  • Any information you share in the free-text field

When we onboard you as a client, we connect to your store platform, ad accounts, and email tools via their official APIs. We access only the data necessary to produce your weekly intelligence report.

3. How we use your data

  • To respond to your demo request and schedule a call
  • To produce your weekly AI-powered decision report
  • To improve the quality and relevance of our analysis over time
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes

4. Data storage and security

Your data is stored securely and encrypted in transit. We retain contact form submissions for up to 12 months. Client store data is retained only for as long as your engagement with us is active, and deleted within 30 days of termination upon request.

5. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@7captur.com.

6. Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics scripts. We do not use advertising pixels or retargeting of any kind.
